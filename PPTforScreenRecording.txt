This is a tutorial for screen recording by powerpoint:
1.open powerpoint(ppt).
2.if you don't have ppt, please install it from internet.
3.choose "insert" and "Screen Recording"
4.and then you can choose the area where you want to recording
5.if you enter "win + shift + f", you can recording full screen fast
6.click a square to stop the recording and media now is on the center of PPT
7.right click and choose save media, you can save the media
Thanks for watching.
